Our Story

Established in 2021 in Kozhikode, N Touch Syndicate began its humble journey as a welding service provider. Over the years, with our commitment to quality, precision, and a customer-centric approach, we expanded our expertise into larger industrial projects, including resort and building construction. What began as a small-scale operation soon evolved into a comprehensive industry leader, and today, N Touch Syndicate stands as a proud, multifaceted enterprise offering a wide range of services across various sectors. We have redefined our presence in the market, not just as a construction company but as a dynamic mother company. Our name change to N Touch Syndicate marks the beginning of a new era, reflecting our growth, diversification, and commitment to offering holistic solutions.
